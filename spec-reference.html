Active blending spec — 2026 EPA national waiver

8.75psiQC blendstock target
9.0psiBlendstock ceiling
10.0psiFinished product ceiling
The 2026 national fuel waiver collapses the country into a single gasoline pool, temporarily lifting the DFW RFG restriction and permitting butane blending at Fort Worth. The QC target is set at the blendstock limit minus 0.25 psi (9.0 − 0.25 = 8.75), settable from the RVP reference workbook. ULSD is unaffected.

Normal regime — DFW Federal RFG

7.4 psiSuspended under waiver
  • •Summer reformulated-gasoline cap of 7.4 psi RVP (Class AAA), DFW 10-county area, mandatory since Nov 7, 2023.
  • •No 1-psi ethanol waiver in RFG areas during the summer control period.
  • •Butane blending normally prohibited in RFG gasoline under 40 CFR 1090.220(e).
  • •Reverts to this regime when the national waiver lapses — segregate lower-RVP CBOB for DFW.
